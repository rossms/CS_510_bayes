Steve Young has to be ranked among the all-time great QBs. Not only was he the best running QB ever, but he had a great touch and was incredibly tough. His reluctance to retire demonstrates how difficult it is for football players and all athletes to hang them up. After all, Young is an attorney who had won everything there is to win in the NFL and he was still hesitant. Imagine how it must be for those players who don't have such great things waiting for them at the ends of their careers.
