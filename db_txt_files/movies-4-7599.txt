Lot's of colorful characters and a good cast with George Clooney, Tim Blake Nelson, John Turturro, Michael Badalucco, John Goodman, Charles Durning, Holly Hunter, Chris Thomas King, and Stephen Root. The characters are rich and colorful. The two standouts are Tim Blake Nelson as Delmar and Michael Badalucco as Baby Face Nelson. Another plus is the music in the movie is part of the story.
