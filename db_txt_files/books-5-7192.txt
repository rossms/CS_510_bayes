This book does not underestimate the reader. It is fascinating, uses technology as a major element in the plot without stooping to a ghost in the maching use of hacking as a plot solver. The way the book weaves three then four different plot planes into one that comes together near the end was masterful The writing style thrilling and describes geeks in a very humane and lovable way. If you're kind of geeky and not completely afraid of math and logic, this book will likely be a great read for you.
