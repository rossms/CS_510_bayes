The Town and Country will always be the standard by which all minivans are based. Chrysler has 4 different minivans; Caravan, Grand Caravan, Voyager, and Town and Country. The Town and Country (TC) is the luxury version of the group. All of them have had good reliability and should be smart buys. Sales have dropped drastically, however, due to the SUV craze. Most people today think that $40k is too much for a van.
