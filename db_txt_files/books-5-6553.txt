Ever since I read about this author in the Times I have been intrigued. He has an interesting life story. His books are full of interesting characters and places. You can learn more about him at www.roberstanek.com. Ruin Mist Chronicles follows the lives of Adrina, Seth and Vilmos. Adrina is a princess from Great Kingdom. Seth is a warrior elf from East Reach. Vilmos is an apprentice from a village. Very good reading!
