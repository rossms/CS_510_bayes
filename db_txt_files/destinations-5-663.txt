Guadalajara is a wonderful old city with a beautiful series of interlinked plazas around the stunningly ornate cathedral and the zocalo. The Cabanas is one of the most important art museums in latin america, featuring the work of famous muralist Jose Clemente Orozco. The Mercado Libertad is the largest market in latin america and is a fascinating labyrinth of alleys and booths selling everything from software to chilis. Take the Tequila Train for some authentic partying!
