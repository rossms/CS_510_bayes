Disney Golf is an excellant game for people of all ages! The graphics are outstandingly beautiful and the charactors are fun! I don't even really like video games but I can't stay away from this one.It is full of imagination and creativity. The courses include a sky course, tropical, western, european, American and many others. Playing this game is like actually being part of the scenary. It is relaxing and fun, I highly reccomend it to anyone!
