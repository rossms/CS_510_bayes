The Escalade ESV is the third version of the Escalade lineup. This includes the regular Escalade, the new ESV, and the truck version, EXT. The regular version is the size of a Tahoe. The EXT is a suped-up Avalanche. The new ESV is based on the Suburban. The ESV starts at $56,000. Its the biggest Cadillac ever produced. It can seat 7 or 8 people, depending on seating arrangments. Look for basic Cadillac luxury, DVD Navigation, LCD moniters, Nauce leather, etc. AWD is standard along with a 6.0L V-8, 345hp.
