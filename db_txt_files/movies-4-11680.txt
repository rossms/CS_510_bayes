A very funny movie directed by Danny DeVito and stars Ben Stiller, Drew Barrymore and Eileen Essel. Stiller and Barrymore play a young couple who move into a Duplex where there upstairs tennant is an old Irish lady named Mrs Connelly who seems like a sweet person but they soon find out how manipulative she can be to them. She makes their lives miserable and they soon plot to get rid of her. Eileen Essel steals the movie as Mrs Connelly.
