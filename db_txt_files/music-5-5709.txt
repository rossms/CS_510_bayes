 This was Metallica when they were hard core Metallica. This amazing CD is heavy metal with melody. A great combination of the two, this album highlights Metallica's songwriting abilities with speed demon riffs and double bass pedal. Much deeper and thoughtful than the gut metal of Kill 'em All, yet more edge than Metallica (the black album . This was one of Metallica's greatest efforts before their sad demise into pop culture in the mid 90's.
