Sweden is a very beautiful country. The city of Stockholm is one of the finest cities in Europe. Built on 13 islands, Stockholm is called a city on water. It is very well preserved. In fact, the medival roots of the city are easily recognized on some of the islands. The coasts are filled with fishing villages and very beautiful beaches, and there are many beautiful forests throughout the country. With a prosperous economy, high standard of living, and small population, Sweden is definately a wonderful travel destination.
