a nice small novel less than 200 pages.An 83 year old man named Eddie is on the last day of his life on Earth. He works for an amusement park a job he has had most of his adult life. He is not very happy thinking he did not have much of a productive life. when he dies trying to save a little girl on a ride. he is met by five people he has known in his life each explains the impact he had on them. He gets an understanding how his life affected others. a bit of a reminder of A Christmas Carol, and It's a Wonderful Life where Eddie gets to see how others knew him.A nice book a quick read. Well written.
