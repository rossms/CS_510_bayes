Now here is one classy lady! Ms. Bacall is easily one of the best American lead actresses of the previous generation, and she has made the transition into smaller supporting roles with the same level of grace and professionalism. All you need do is check out this great actresses earlier work, especially The Big Sleep, Key Largo, Dark Passage, To Have and Have Not, and How to Marry a Millionare, to see her skills. And you can see why an older Humphry Bogart fell so hard for her! A classic American actress!
