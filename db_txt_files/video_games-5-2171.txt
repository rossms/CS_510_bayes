You know you can have a successful game franchise when the predicessors to the original can prove to be superior to the original product. In all aspects, Mario Kart 64 is that much superior to the SuperNES classic and then some. Much of the beloved original gameplay is incorporated into this sequel along with great additions such as more unique and fun races, and more luscious landscapes. The multiplayer mode on this game became on of the first hints to game makers that group games will sell tremendously.
