Montreal's casino, rated in the top 10 category of the largest casinos in North America, triggers some nostalgic emotions in native Montrealers. This is because the casino is housed in the renovated American and French pavillions that marked Montreal's hosting of the 1967 Expo world fair. Complete with several floors of gambling space, beautiful views, glass elevators and floors, restaurants and shows, this casino is a sure way to have a great time!
