I have known Connie for over fifty years. He is without a doubt the best player I have ever seen. I have seen him play since he was in junior high school and watched him do things I have never seen anyone else do. After he entered the NBA and had played a few games I asked him when was he going to really show the fans and players what he could really do. His response was I can't do all of that stuff because those guys would hurt me. Can you imagine? Here is a guy who had to tone down his game to play in the NBA because he was afraid of embarassing the other players. The Hawk was undoubtedly the greatest
