A delicious, cinnamony, caffeine-free herbal infusion. A small amount of sugar gives it 18 calories per 16 oz. bottle. Cinnamon dominates the flavor, but it also includes ginger, peppermint, chamomile, rosehips, rooibos, and orange peel. According to the label, the name Gold Rush Cinnamon refers to the recipe's California origins (though they didn't say whether it was a traditional recipe or how far back it dates).
