Bob Feller of the Cleveland Indians (1936 - 1941, 1945 - 1956) pitched three no-hit games in A.L., 12 one hit games, set modern record strikeout record with 18 in a game for that time, struck out 348 batters in one of his best seasons, led A.L. in victories six times. Bob Feller hadd 266 wins, 162 looses, 2581 Strikeouts, and an ERA of 3.25.
