Big Daddy is the first Adam Sandler movie that has a lot of heartwarming humor in it, and that's really good. It's an extremly funny movie about a man who is left to look after a 6 year-old boy, after his mother finds out that she is dying from cancer. Their relationship grows, until Social Services wants to take him away. Even people wqho don't care for crude humor, will find something to enjoy about this comedy.
