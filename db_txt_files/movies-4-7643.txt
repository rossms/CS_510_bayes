Two newlyweds die in a car accident. they go back to their house to find that the realtor friend of theirs has sold it to this family, an obnoxious wife art person, stuffy husband, and teenage daughter. the couple are now ghosts and the only person who can see them is the girl. they see an ad for this ghost named Beetlejuice who promises to get this family out of the house. Michael Keaton is great as beetlejuice the unconventional ghost.He plays some parlor tricks on the family as they are entertaining a group very funny scene.In the end they find a way to live together. has some very funny moments.
