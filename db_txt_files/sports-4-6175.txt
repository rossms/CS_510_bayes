Kyle Lowry is ferocious. Anyone who saw Lowry decimate St. Joe's last night in the Big 5 championship knows what I'm talking about. At 6'0 (barely), he pounds the glass, will dunk on fast breaks, and is impossible to keep out of the paint. But it's his defense where he's exceptional. He leads the Big East in steals, and doesn't ever let the ballhandler breathe. Watch out if his J ever develops...
