This game is perhaps one of the best fighting titles along with Capcom vs. SNK on the Dreamcast platform. This is the sequel game to the original, which introduced the concept of having female characters with bouncing breasts. Ability of multiplayer feature (up to 4 people), which is unavailable even on PlayStation 2 version of the game. Features significantly unnoticeable loading time, and has been reviewed by video game magazine as being a better arcade port over the PS2 version.
