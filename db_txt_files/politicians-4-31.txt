If you had to live out your life in the Royal Family gold fish bowl, this wouldn't be the worst spot to be in. Unless they all manage to shuffle off at once, he's never going to have to assume the big title, become paparazzi chow, a major tabloid victim, or do much of anything he doesnt really want to do. Its like a royal dodge ball game and Edward is the pale, low key, skinny kid nobody thinks of eliminating until the end. His strategy is no strategy at all. But, it seems to be working just the same.
