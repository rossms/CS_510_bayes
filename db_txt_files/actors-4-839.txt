Paco Rabal's prominence in European productions was established early on and his memorable performances were observed by directors from Luis Buñuel ('Nazarin', 1959; 'Viridiana', 1959 and 'Belle de Jour', 1968) to Michelangelo Antonioni ('L'Eclisse', 1962) and Pedro Almodóvar ('Tie me Up! Tie Me Down!', 1990). A Cannes Film Festival winner for 'Los Santos Inocentes', 1984)
