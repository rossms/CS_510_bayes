Yahtzee can be fun. It is a quick game of chance. With three roles of the dice you have to decide what to do with them. there is a score pad where you have to try and get everything on there. A Yahtzee is five of a kind. and the highest of the points. Other scores to attempt are four of a kind. full house. There is some straegy involved and just plain taking your chances.
