A joyous heart-warming Oscar winner ,with special appeal for lovers of delicious food! In the 1870's,in Denmark's bleak wintry Jutland peninsula,a French refugee servant of 2 elderly spinsters surprises her austere religious friends (who are unaware she was once a renowned Parisian chef!) with the gift of a sumptuous feast.As sensitively crafted as the culinary fare on display, and glowing with a magical spirit of generosity and love.
