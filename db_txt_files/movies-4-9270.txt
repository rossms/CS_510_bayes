Uncle Buck comes to take care of his neices and nephew while their parents leave town for an emergency. He sees very little of the kids so they do not know each other very well. His 15 year old niece has an attitude problem and a jerk of a boyfriend.John Candy is great as Uncle Buck who has his own way of dealing with the kids. especially his snotty teenage niece. there are some real funny scenes. this is one of MacCauley Culkins first movies. One of John Candy best roles.
