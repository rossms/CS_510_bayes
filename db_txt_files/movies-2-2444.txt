Roger Moore did a credible job as Bond. Good sense of humor; Moore's characterization of the super spy borrowed from his role as Simon in the 60's Saint series. Live and Let Die however can best be described as one of the worst (in my opinion) of this venerable cinematic franchise. Most Bond films no matter who was 007 were virtually timeless. But, L L Die is extremely dated. You know it is the godawful early 70's. Cheesy effects and sets make this one a tedius dog to watch. Up till this one, the soundtracks were outstanding (JohnBarry) not this one
