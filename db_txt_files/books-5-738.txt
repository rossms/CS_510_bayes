Now here's a discovery well worth making!Chester,a Connecticut country cricket,finds himself accidentally transported to the Big Apple, where he is befriended by a young boy Mario, Harry the Cat and Tucker the Mouse.His wonderful musicianship soon attracts admirers to the news-stand of Mario's family.With appeal for a wide range of ages(from 7 upwards),certainly one of the most endearing of all children's books,and an absolute treat.
