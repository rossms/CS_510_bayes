Denmark is one of the most beautiful countries in the world. Copenhagen is filled with wonderful tourist attractions, the most famous of which is Tivoli Gardens, a very beautiful amusment park. The island of Funen has some of the most beautiful scenery in the world. The Jutland peninsula is mostly beaches, but there are two large cities, Aalborg and Arhus. In addition, Legoland lies in the small town of Billund. The Danes have good character and an interseting lifestyle.
