Tom Chambers helped transform Suns into a contender. In the 1988-89 season, he lead the Suns to 27-win turnaround, third biggest in league history The Suns advanced to Conference Finals three times and NBA Finals once during Chambers' five seasons in Phoenix Chambers is one of Suns best scorers, scoring the Suns record 60 points versus Seattle on March 24, 1990, averaging 27.2 per game in 1989-90. He is also a Four-time NBA All-Star, three as a Sun.
