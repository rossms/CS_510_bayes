A nicely done little movie.A 30ish woman who's family is Greek.Decides she wants to change her life, so she Changes her looks and dress style, goes back to college and changes jobs. {for years she worked at her family's Greek restaurant] She meets a man who is not Greek, they fall in love and want to marry. At first her family especially her dad is not happy. They want her to marry a Greek man.Eventually they accept her man even though he is not greek. this is a very sweet movie well acted and funny. The lead actress takes from her own experiences of growing up in a Greek family.
