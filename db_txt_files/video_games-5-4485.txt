This is a unique game that allows you to become anyone you want to be. It lets you live out the life of the sims of your choice. You design the sim, you give them life, you can control their every move. Or you can allow them to live freestyle and only control partial aspects of their life. To me, the concept is awesome. The homebuilder is easy to understand and fun to use, and everyday there is something new designed by either maxis or a user made item you can download. Or create your own. Live life to the fullest. Sim Style!
