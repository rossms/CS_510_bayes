Ron Ross takes the classic W.C Heinz feature story,Brownsville Bum about the colorful and tragically short life of the hard hitting boxer Al Bummy Davis to the next level-up. Davis, who was recently rated 54th of the 100 best pound-for-pound punchers ever never backed down from anyone, including Murder Incorporated.Davis, who died preventing a bar stickup with his fists is given the royal treatment he deserves by Ross in a knockout book.
