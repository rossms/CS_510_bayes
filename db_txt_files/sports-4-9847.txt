They are definitely a fun team to watch. Yao Ming has become a major attraction and his inordinate popularity was displayed through the fact that he got the starting spot on the All-Star team. They have the best success when the work the ball through Yao where he can make a post move or draw the double team and utilize his passing ability to dish it to Glen Rice, Francis, or Mobley for the jumper. The big fella was certainly a positive acquisition to the Rockets' squad.
