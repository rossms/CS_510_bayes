This was probably one of the first chocolate bar I've ever had. In any case, I remember it as the first which I would call 'my favorite'. It isn't anymore and hasn't been for a long time but Crunch's are still a very good chocolate bar. Its simply milk chocolate mixed in with rice krispies but the crunch and sweetness of it all along with the slight soggyness of the cereal that occurs if you suck on a piece to make it melt is amazing. A very good bar for when you don't want all the trimmings but just the basis
