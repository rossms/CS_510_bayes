This beer surprised me, we just got back from a cruise that stopped in the Dominican Republic. Dominican Republic seemed to be generally impoverished, so my hopes of finding a good beer were not high. Although, maybe it had something to do with it being 85 degrees there and me being from the northeast where it was still fairly cool, just about any beer would taste good in that situation! Serioualy though, Presidente is worth a look if you have the opportunity
