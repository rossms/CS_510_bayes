One of the so-called killer apps for the Dreamcast, Sonic Adventure is a great game all-around. The graphics flaunt the underrated power of the Dreamcast, which has been proven to exceed that of the Playstation II IN CERTAIN PORTIONS. The voice acting is great and is very much like the kind you find in saturday morning toons, which is great! The game has many playable levels and characters. Also, the controls respond on a dime. I only wish it had the bonus levels like Sonic 3D Blast on Saturn had...
