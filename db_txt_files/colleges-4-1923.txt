If you want to experience a true college town Pullman, WA is the place to be. Nestled in the rolling hills of the Palouse, Wazzu has the intimacy you look for in a small school with all the amenities of a big Pac-10 University. While it has had the honor of being off-the chart on Playboy's annual Party-school rankings, it has a very underrated academics and athletic departments. Go Cougs!
