Reigned for fifty years, gaining a reputation as one of the finest military commander of his age. Unsuccesfully claimed the throne of France against the rival candidate Philip VI, founder of the Valois dynasty. Started the Hundred Years' War, founder of the Order of the Garter. Known both for chivalory and for womanizing. The descedants of his six sons include both the Lancasters and the Yorks who would become responsible for the Wars of the Roses. The only of his descedants to match his achievements in battle was probably Henry V.
