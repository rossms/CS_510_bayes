At the time they were drafted, Jamison was more highly regarded than Vince Carter. Through the first half dozen years of their NBA careers, most people would speculate that it was a serious miscalculation to have drafted him before Air Canada. During the last season or two, however, 'Tawn has been the better player. It will be interesting to see how these two former Tarheel teammates stack up against each other when they get to the end of their playing careers in the NBA.
