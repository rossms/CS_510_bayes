Warren Spahn became the fifth biggest winner in Major League history with 363 victories. Most voctories for a left-hander. Won 20 or more games 13 seasons, six in a row. Set the all-time records for years leading league in victories (8) and complete games (9). Also N.L. career highs with 665 games started; 5264 innings; 2,853 strikeouts. Pitched a no-hitter in 1960 and another in 1961. Warren Spahn played from 1942 - 1965.
