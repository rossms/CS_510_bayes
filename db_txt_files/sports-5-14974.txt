A late round draft pick that inspired the comment Joe Who? Greene was a menacing force in the famous Steel Curtain defense of the Steeler Super Bowl years. From his defensive tackle spot, Greene intimidated offensive lineman for his 10+ years in the NFL. Almost always doubleteamed, Greene allowed linebackers Ham, and Lambert to enhance their reputations. Quite arguably one of the best three or four defensive lineman to ever play the game.
