With a blazing fastball that approached 100 mph and a work ethic like no other, Nolan Ryan dominated hitters for an unparalleled 27 seasons on his way to an all-time record 5,714 strikeouts. During four decades of prominence, he totaled 324 victories and a host of a major league records. Most notable of his milestones are a mystifying seven no-hitters and 12 one hitters, The eight-time All-Star fanned a record 383 batters in 1973, and his career strikeouts encompassed 1,176 different players. Nolan Ryan was inducted into the Hall of Fame in 1999.
