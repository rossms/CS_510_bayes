The album that lauch two new genres of heavy metal (thrash and speed). The five men that contributed to this album(James Hetfield, Lars Ulrich, Kirk Hammett, Cliff Burton, and Dave Mustaine{though no longer in the group at this point in time, he co wrote 4 metal classics}) would help forever seal metal's foundation in music. This album is filled with classics (stand outs include Four Horsemen, Whiplash, No Remorse , and Seek and Destroy )
