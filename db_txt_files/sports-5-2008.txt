The New York Yankees are the greatest professional franchise in all of sports. No other team has dominated as much as they have. Sure, the Canadiens have 23 titles. But think of this. Every time the Yanks have won, they had to go through a 154 or 162 game season, year after year. They have done it consistently. Since 1923, the longest they have gone without a championship was 18 years. Since that dry spell, they have won 3 out of 4. That spells greatness
