Mr. Guiliani is one of the most courageous people to ever live. Although brash at times, his ethics, compassion, and bravery are unmatched. He was a staunch supporter of the civil services in New York City, and as a result, he successfully did what all New Yorkers wanted him to do, reduce crime while increasing overall security in the city. Tourism to the city steadily grew under his tutelage. Having a powerful figure such as Mayor Guiliani was a major stabilizing force to the city of New York.
