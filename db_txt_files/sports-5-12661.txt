In the areas of fans,tradition, and success, the Huskers are the premier football program in America. Memorial Stadium has been sold out for over 267 consecutive games, dating back to the Nov. 3, 1962, contest against Missouri. Nebraska continues to lead the nation in cumulative football CoSIDA First-Team Academic All-American selections. The selections are made by the College Sports Information Directors of America.
