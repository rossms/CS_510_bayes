Johnston gives a layman's explanation of the United States tax system and many of its flaws. Plenty of names are given to identify some of the biggest cheapskates in our country. There are loopholes that allow the mega-rich to live their lives practically tax free. Infomation about the Alternative Minimum Tax (which is currently being considered for revision in Cogress). How use of corporate jets is used as a tax dodge and costs the country much more than you would think. Offshore tax havens, CEO greed, estate tax, etc. The best Non-Fiction I've read in the last five years.
