This Antique Mall is one of the largest in Nevada. It has ample parking and is wheelchair accessible. The management and most of the staff formerly worked for the Sampler Shoppes of Las Vegas, which lost its lease in January 2005 and which always was highly rated in annual Las Vegas Review Journal Best of Las Vegas ratings.
