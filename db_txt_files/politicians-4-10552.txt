Do I agree with everything he says? No, of course not. No one does. But he is our President. Is he a smooth talking, eloquent guy? No, not necessarily. He may not have the Clinton way of speaking and presenting himself, but I admire a straightforward man. Let's face it. We would not want to be him right now, and we are so quick to criticize.
