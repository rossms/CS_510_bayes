Al Kaline of the Detriot Tigers was the twelfth player to reach elite 3,000-hit plateau. He socked 399 homers and attained .297 career average, with nine years in .300 class. Finished in all-time top 15 with 2,834 games, 3,007 hits, 1583 RBI's, 4,852 total bases. Played 100 or more games 20 years and had 242 consecutive errorless games in outfield, 1970 - 1972, for A.L. records. Led in hits and won batting title in 1955 at age 20.
