Juan Marichal of the San Francisco Giants (1960 - 1973) was a high kicking right-hander from dominican republic won 243 games and lost only 142 over 16 seasons. Won 20 games six times and a no-hitter in Houston in 1963. Led N.L. in complete games and shutouts twice and in ERA with 2.10 in 1969. Completed 244 games during his career, striking out 2,303 and finishing with a 2.89 ERA.
