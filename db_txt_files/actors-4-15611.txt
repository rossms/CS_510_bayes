Mimi Gibson was born in Washington state, and her stage mother took her to Hollywood at a very young age. An adorable little blonde with good acting ability she appeared in an enormous number of movies through the 1950s. Most notable was her role as Cary Grant's daughter in Houseboat {1957}. Her mother took all her movie earnings, leaving her an age 18 virtually penniless, though she had earned alot of money from her many movie roles.
