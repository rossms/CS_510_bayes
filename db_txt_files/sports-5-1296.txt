Trot Nixon is one of the more underrated baseball players. Though he is not very quick, he makes up for his lack of speed while using good decision making as a right fielder and he has a very strong arm. At the plate, he has power and hits slightly better than average. Nixon showed his true colors in the playoffs in 2003 playing hard all the time and hitting clutch home runs. Nixon does not personify most players on the Red Sox, past or present
