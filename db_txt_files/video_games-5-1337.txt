As usual, the Mario Kart series on any nintendo system proves to be a classic. The only new changes you'll find in this game is different map for GP and battle modes, and a fifth cup in GP mode. Otherwise , it's the same characters and gameply from both the N64 and Super NES versions. However, that doesn't mean you won't be playing from hours and hours till the end of time. As always, great 4 player action. Also, as a special bonus, you can unlock all the old trackss from Super Mario Kart. This is a checking out, especially with a couple of buds
